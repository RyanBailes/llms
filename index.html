Frequently Asked Questions

LLMS.txt is a standardized text file that helps AI systems understand and index your website content. It provides metadata about your site, similar to how robots.txt works for search engines but optimized for large language models.

Shopify doesn't allow merchants to upload files to their store's root directory where AI crawlers expect to find LLMS.txt. Our service provides an alternative location that follows AI crawling standards while working within Shopify's platform limitations.

While we can't guarantee placement in AI results, having properly formatted and accessible LLMS.txt files significantly increases the chances that your products and content will be discovered and included in AI-generated responses.

Sitemap.xml helps traditional search engines understand your site structure, while LLMS.txt provides additional metadata specifically designed for AI systems. They serve complementary purposes, and we recommend having both for maximum visibility.
