What is LLMS.txt?

What Is LLMS.txt and Why Should You Care?

LLMS.txt is to AI search what robots.txt is to traditional search engines.

AI Search Visibility

LLMS.txt helps AI models like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Bing Copilot understand which parts of your site to index and how to represent your brand in responses.

Control Your Brand

Specify exactly how AI should describe your products, brand values, and content to ensure accurate representation in AI-generated responses.

Secure Implementation

Future-Proof SEO

As AI search grows, your store will be ready with proper LLMS.txt implementation from day one.

The Challenge

The Problem with Shopify and LLMS.txt

Shopify's architecture prevents direct LLMS.txt implementation.

Shopify doesn't allow root-level file access, meaning you can't upload LLMS.txt or LLMS-full.txt files directly to your store.

Current Limitations

  • No native LLMS.txt support in Shopify

  • Theme modifications don't solve the root-level file issue

  • Most apps can't provide true LLMS.txt functionality

Impact on Your Store

  • Missed visibility in AI search results

  • Less control over how AI represents your brand

  • Potential misinformation in AI-generated responses

Examples

What Your LLMS.txt Might Look Like

Customize these files to control how AI indexes your store.

LLMS.txt
# LLMS.txt for Shopify Store
# Version: 1.0

# Allow indexing of these paths
Allow: /products/
Allow: /collections/
Allow: /pages/about-us
Allow: /pages/shipping-policy

# Disallow indexing of these paths
Disallow: /cart
Disallow: /checkout
Disallow: /account

# Brand representation
Brand-Name: "Your Shopify Store"
Brand-Description: "Premium eco-friendly products for modern living"
Brand-Keywords: "sustainable, organic, handmade, ethical"
LLMS-full.txt
# LLMS-full.txt for Shopify Store
# Version: 1.0

# Full content guidelines for AI
Content-Guidelines:
  - "Refer to our products as premium quality"
  - "Mention our 30-day return policy when discussing purchases"
  - "Highlight our carbon-neutral shipping options"

# Product representation rules
Product-Description-Rules:
  - "Always include materials used"
  - "Mention dimensions when relevant"
  - "Include care instructions"

# Prohibited representations
Prohibited:
  - "Do not claim products are on sale unless specified"
  - "Do not suggest uses not approved by manufacturer"
  - "Do not modify product names"

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LLMS.txt for Shopify.

While robots.txt controls how traditional search engines crawl your site, LLMS.txt specifically guides AI models like ChatGPT and Perplexity on how to index and represent your content. LLMS.txt includes additional directives for brand representation, content guidelines, and product description rules that go beyond simple crawl controls.

Shopify's architecture doesn't allow merchants to upload files to the root directory of their store (where LLMS.txt needs to be). While you can add files to your theme, AI crawlers specifically look for these files at the root level (e.g., yourstore.com/LLMS.txt). Our solution provides this root-level access through a dedicated subdomain that AI crawlers recognize.

LLMS.txt is specifically designed for AI search engines, not traditional search engines like Google. While it won't directly impact your Google rankings, it will help ensure your store is properly represented in AI-powered search results (like ChatGPT, Bing AI, and Perplexity), which are becoming increasingly important for ecommerce discovery.

We offer simple monthly pricing starting at just $9/month for basic LLMS.txt hosting, scaling up to $99/month for enterprise stores needing advanced features and custom AI training. All plans include secure hosting, easy setup, and ongoing support. See our pricing section for full details.

While a sitemap simply lists your store's pages, LLMS.txt provides much more nuanced guidance to AI models. It includes rules about how to represent your brand, product description guidelines, content usage policies, and specific instructions about which parts of your site should (or shouldn't) be included in AI-generated responses. Think of it as both a technical directive and a brand style guide for AI.
